About

TOOL is a research and production studio working at the intersection of engineering, craft, and technology. We take on projects that require both deep thinking and precise making.

Our process begins with rigorous R&D — understanding the problem fully before touching materials or code. The result is work that is not just functional, but considered.

We work with clients who value quality over speed, and who understand that the best solutions are rarely the most obvious ones.
